🛒 ShopNest Retail Business Analysis (Power BI)
📌 Project Objective
Analyze e-commerce retail data to identify revenue trends, seasonal patterns, delivery performance, product ratings, and regional sales distribution.

🗂️ Dataset Overview:
This project uses multiple datasets including:
Customers
Orders
Order Items
Payments
Reviews
Products
Sellers
Geolocation

📊 Key Business Questions Solved:
Top 10 product categories by revenue
Delayed orders analysis
Monthly delayed vs on-time comparison
Product rating insights
State-wise sales performance
Seasonal sales patterns
Yearly revenue growth

📈 Key Insights:
Revenue shows steady growth across years
Strong seasonal spikes observed in Q4
Certain categories have higher delivery delays
Top-performing states dominate overall sales
Lower-rated products often correlate with delivery issues

🛠️ Tools & Skills Used:
Power BI (Data Modeling & Visualization)
DAX (Measures & Time Intelligence)
Star Schema Design
Business Analytics & KPI Development
